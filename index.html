Data-Driven Grading Intelligence

Gem Chase provides a structured look at the sports card market for collectors who buy raw and sell graded. By processing price gaps, gem rates, and seasonal market trends, the app identifies cards with significant spreads between raw and PSA-graded values. Our goal is to replace "gut feel" with a systematic approach, using an algorithm that weighs liquidity and market timing to help you organize your next grading submission.

Analytical Tools for Flippers

Built for speed and accuracy, Gem Chase includes a net-return simulator that factors in marketplace fees, grading costs, and time to grade. Beyond the numbers, the platform offers specialized views like "Mint Yields" for cards that show profit potential at a PSA 9 grade, and "Top Picks" for cards with high market velocity. It is a streamlined dashboard designed to help you track price movement and manage your inventory with professional-grade data.
