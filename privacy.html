GEM CHASE – PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. Overview

Gem Chase ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Gem Chase mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We prioritize data minimization. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal identifiers like your name or phone number. However, to provide subscription services, we collect limited data:

• Purchase and Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you subscribe, RevenueCat collects a unique, anonymous "App User ID" and your purchase history (receipt data). This allows us to verify your "Pro" status and provide access to premium features across your devices.

• Technical & Usage Data: We collect anonymous technical information, such as your device model, operating system version, and approximate location (locale/country) to ensure the App performs correctly and to analyze market trends by region.

• Customer Support: If you contact us via email, we use your email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. Payment Processing

All financial transactions are handled exclusively by the Apple App Store. Gem Chase does not see, collect, or store your credit card numbers, billing addresses, or bank information. Your payments are subject to the Apple Media Services Terms and Conditions.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We share limited, anonymous data with the following service providers to keep the app running:

• RevenueCat: Used to process and validate subscriptions. You can view their privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

• Market Data Providers: The App displays market data from third-party sources (e.g., Market Movers). These providers do not receive any of your personal data from us.

5. Data Retention and Security

Since we do not collect personal identities, we do not store "profiles." Subscription data is retained by RevenueCat as long as your subscription is active. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the anonymous data we do process.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (such as the EU under GDPR or California under CCPA), you may have the right to access or delete data held about you. Because we use anonymous identifiers, we may require your RevenueCat App User ID (found in the App settings) to process these requests.

7. Third-Party Links

The App contains links to external platforms like eBay or PSA. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: info@gemchase.com
